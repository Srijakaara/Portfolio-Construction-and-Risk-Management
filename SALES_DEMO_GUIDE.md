# PortfolioRiskAI (Ascend Risk AI) — Sales & Demo Guide
*For Sales / Business / Demo teams · Plain-language · Demo runs on simulated data, no live market feed.*

**1. Project Overview:** PortfolioRiskAI helps Asset Managers build portfolios that adapt to changing markets instead of running on a fixed model forever. It detects regime shifts, recommends allocation changes, stress-tests continuously, and learns from every cycle — built because static models cause repeated underperformance after market shifts, and a human always stays in control of the decision.

**2. Problem Statement:** AMCs rely on static allocation models that don't adapt; regime changes (rate cycles, volatility shocks) are spotted late, causing 12–18 month underperformance; risk assessment and stress testing are manual, periodic chores; portfolios are slow to adapt to new conditions.

**3. Solution — 4 Pillars:** **Regime Detection** — AI spots market shifts early. **Dynamic Allocation** — recommendations adapt to the current regime, not a fixed model. **Stress + Scenario Engine** — Monte Carlo/stress tests run weekly, not monthly. **Risk Memory** — every event learned from, so the system gets smarter each cycle. *Core rule: AI proposes, a human decides — every decision is logged.*

**4. Project Workflow:** Market data ingestion → Preprocessing & feature engineering → Regime detection → Risk assessment & scoring → Allocation recommendations → Human review (auto-approve / Workbench / escalate) → Performance monitoring → Dashboard visualization & reporting.

**6. Key Features:** Regime detection · Dynamic allocation · Continuous stress testing · Explainable, confidence-scored recommendations · Configurable autonomy thresholds · SLA-tracked review workbench · Full audit trail · Role-based dashboards · AI model governance registry · Live integration health.

**7. Business Benefits:** **AMCs** — less regime-shift underperformance, lower manual risk-ops cost, stronger audit posture. **Fund Managers** — earlier warnings, explainable recommendations, faster action. **Risk Teams** — continuous testing, prioritized queue, permanent record. **Investors** — steadier downside protection, transparency, human-governed AI.

**8. Competitive Advantages:** Adaptive, not static · Continuous, not periodic · Explainable, not a black box · Client-governed autonomy, not autonomous-by-default · Compounding improvement over time · Audit-ready by design, not bolted on later.

**9. Example Client Pitch (30 sec):** *"Your portfolio model is static — when the market regime shifts, it doesn't notice in time, costing 12–18 months of underperformance. Ascend Risk AI detects the shift early, recommends the right adjustment, stress-tests weekly instead of monthly, and gets smarter every cycle — but it never acts alone on anything risky. **10. Architecture Overview:** Data Sources → AI Engine (regime & allocation models) → Risk Engine (stress testing & scoring) → Portfolio Optimization Module → Dashboard/UI Layer (human review & governance — nothing acts above the autonomy threshold without a person).
